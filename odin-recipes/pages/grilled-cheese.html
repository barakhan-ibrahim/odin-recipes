Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a basic grilled cheese recipe. The items needed are the following:

  • White bread.
  • Sliced cheese, mozaerella or cheddar works best.
  • Butter or margarine

Here are the preparation steps:

  1. Heat a non stick pan
  2. Take two slices of bread, place a slice of cheese between them
  3. Butter both sides, place on pan
  4. Flip once brown
  5. Don't burn it.
