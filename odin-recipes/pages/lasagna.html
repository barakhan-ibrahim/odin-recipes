Lasagna

Description

This is a basic lasagna recipe found on the internet. The items needed are the following:

  • Ground beef or ground turkey.
  • Spaghetti sauce or tomato sauce
  • Eggs to bind the mixture
  • Mozarella, parmesan, and cottage cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup Water

Here are the preparation steps:

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, and then stir in the sauce. Leave to simmer
  2. Combine cheese, seasonings, and eggs
  3. Assemble the lasagna and and pour water
  4. Bake covered for 45 min
  5. Uncover and bake for 10 min
