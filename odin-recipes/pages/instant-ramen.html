Instant Ramen

Description

This is a basic instant ramen recipe, if you would like to consider it to be one. The items needed are the following:

  • Instant noodle pack.
  • Green onion.
  • 1 egg

Here are the preparation steps:

  1. Boil water in small pot
  2. Place noodle and seasoning in water
  3. Once noodles half cooked, crack egg on top of noodles
  4. Once cooked pour in bowl
  5. Chop green onions place on top of noodles.
